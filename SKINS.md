# Skins

Optional player skins for each origin, grounded in each figure's actual mythology. These aren't
part of the mod itself — a skin is an account-level choice in Minecraft (upload via
minecraft.net or your launcher), not something a mod can apply automatically. Grab whichever
file(s) you like from [`skins/`](skins/) and upload them as a **slim (Alex) model** skin.

Original, from-scratch pixel art built for this project (no licensing concerns), not
professional skin-artist quality, but thematically clear. If you want something more detailed,
the curated links below point to real community art instead.

## Arachne — [`skins/arachne.png`](skins/arachne.png)

Dark chitin-toned skin and limbs, near-black hair, a fitted teal-green outfit with a deep red
center trim (the same accent colors already used on Fang's poison-coated tip), small spider-leg
silhouettes across the back. Red eyes for the venomous-bite theme.

Other options: [Arachne — Queen of Spiders](https://www.planetminecraft.com/mob-skin/arachne-queen-of-spiders/),
the [Arachne skin tag on Planet Minecraft](https://www.planetminecraft.com/skins/tag/arachne/).

## Medusa — [`skins/medusa.png`](skins/medusa.png)

The popular image, not the older monstrous Archaic-Greek Gorgon: a human woman with green snake
hair (a few individual strands hanging loose) and golden petrifying eyes, in a dark stone-grey
dress with a gold collar and trim.

Other options: [Medusa Gorgon (Soul Eater)](https://www.planetminecraft.com/skin/medusa-gorgon-soul-eater/),
[Gorgon Medusa](https://www.planetminecraft.com/skin/gorgon-medusa/),
[Medusa the Gorgon](https://www.minecraftskins.com/skin/11632409/medusa-the-gorgon/).

## Harpy — [`skins/harpy.png`](skins/harpy.png)

Matches this origin's own already-elegant take (a real barn owl screech, graceful flight) rather
than the grittier "filthy vulture-woman" of the original myth: warm barn-owl brown/tan feather
tones, a cream underside, storm-grey collar and leg trim (for the "storm-wind" flavor text), a
speckled feather pattern on the sleeves.

Other options: [Valkie the Harpy](https://www.minecraftskins.com/skin/15417971/valkie-the-harpy/),
the [Harpy skin tag on Planet Minecraft](https://www.planetminecraft.com/skins/tag/harpy) (~78
skins).

## Siren — [`skins/siren.png`](skins/siren.png)

The real Greek myth Siren is actually a bird-woman, not a fish-tailed mermaid (that's a later
medieval conflation) — but this origin's own kit was built explicitly aquatic/mermaid-themed
(swim speed, water breathing, a trident), so the skin follows the kit: pale seafoam-tinted skin,
teal flowing hair, a shell top with gold trim and pearl accents (matching the Crown's Heart of the
Sea), a scaled teal pattern down the legs suggesting a tail.

Other options: [Siren/Mermaid](https://www.planetminecraft.com/skin/sirenmermaid/),
the [siren mermaid search on minecraftskins.com](https://www.minecraftskins.com/search/skin/siren-mermaid/1/).
